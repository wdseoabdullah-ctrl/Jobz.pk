# Jobz.pk – Jobs in Pakistan Portal

Welcome to the Jobz.pk repository. This project represents one of Pakistan’s leading online job platforms, providing daily updates of job opportunities across multiple industries and regions.

# 🌐 About Jobz.pk

Jobz.pk is a popular job portal in Pakistan that publishes the latest government, private, and international job opportunities. It collects listings from newspapers, employers, and recruitment sources to help job seekers find the right career path. ()

The platform serves students, fresh graduates, professionals, freelancers, and skilled workers by offering categorized job listings and career resources.
